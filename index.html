WELCOME TO ANKUR GROUP

Ankur Group is India's leading luxury real estate developer, renowned for their iconic and award-winning projects. Since 2002, Ankur Group has successfully delivered over 39 million sq.ft. of prime real estate, with 41.5 million sq.ft. under development across multiple asset classes that include high-end residential developments, office parks, data centres, build-to-suit office spaces and special economic zones (SEZ). Ankur Group's portfolio includes landmarks such as Trump Towers Pune, YOO Pune, yoo villas, The Ritz-Carlton, Pune and EON Free Zone. Ankur Group is creating world-class real estate by bringing global architects, world-class partners and technology to India.
