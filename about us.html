THE ANKUR GROUP STORY

A LEGACY OF EXCELLENCE

Founded in 2002, Ankur Group is one of India's finest luxury real estate developers. Renowned for its leadership and excellence in real estate development, Ankur Group's portfolio spans across high-end residential developments, IT parks, built-to-suit office spaces, special economic zones (SEZ), hotels, convention centres and luxury retail malls.



Our developments are synonymous with globally benchmarked quality, forward-thinking innovation, and an unwavering commitment to sustainability. By partnering with global giants like The Trump Organization, YOO, Marriott International, and World Trade Center Association, we continue to shape skylines and elevate lifestyles.

Our Vision

To be the world’s most admired real estate company, consistently delivering extraordinary spaces that inspire and empower people to achieve their true potential.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to building iconic, sustainable, and technologically advanced developments that redefine luxury and create long-term value for our stakeholders and the community.

Sustainability
